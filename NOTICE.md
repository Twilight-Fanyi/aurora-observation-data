# Notice and data attribution

Aurora Observation Data

This repository produces derived aurora-observation snapshots from the following upstream services:

- Space-weather products: NOAA Space Weather Prediction Center (SWPC), <https://www.swpc.noaa.gov/>.
- Weather forecasts: Open-Meteo, <https://open-meteo.com/>. Weather data is provided under Open-Meteo's applicable attribution and licensing terms, including CC BY 4.0 where stated by the provider.

The published `catalog.json` also carries machine-readable source names and URLs. Redistributors should preserve this notice and the source metadata.

NOAA and Open-Meteo do not endorse this project. The 0–100 observation score, confidence, reason codes, darkness value, and best window are derived by this project and are not official forecasts or probabilities.
